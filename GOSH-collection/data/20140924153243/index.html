Websites - Forums

Discussion Forums

When I was first self-diagnosed with ADD, reading ADHD forums was a complete revelation. All these other people like me, similar problems but also a similar sense of humour, world views, habits and minds. Living with ADD can seem lonely, finding people with the same challenges, traits and approach to life as yourself can be a fantastic relief and very reassuring. You can discuss all manner of things ADD, from which medications help best, to the latest theories on ADD, to what music people like, to how to get by at work. It is a truly great way to  understand yourself, through seeing yourself in others.
