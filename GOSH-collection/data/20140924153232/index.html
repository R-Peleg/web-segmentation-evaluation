Website and Forum Support

Websites and forums can also help to spread the word about the Big Fish Campaign, and help make hobbyists more aware of the problems that can arise if fish are purchased without researching their potential size first.

If you are a website owner/admin, you can help by placing links to bigfishcampaign.org on your pages and forums.
