About

Spring Valley is a populated place located in Clark county Nevada in the United States of America. It is at 36.1080258 latitude and -115.2450006 longitude. Spring Valley is at an elevation of 721 ft (2365 m). The USGS map that contains this location is titled "Las Vegas SW".
