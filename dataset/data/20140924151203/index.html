About

The IST Elementary library is open Monday – Friday on school days from

7:00 – 3:00

We encourage check-out before and after school, during first break, and anytime the class teacher can send or bring kids!!

The library is open during the June – July 2013 school break from

8:00 – 1:00 Monday – Friday
