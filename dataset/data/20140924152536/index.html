The enterprise credible cloud

Azure Websites is an enterprise-class cloud service for developing, testing, and running web apps. Build apps using your favorite tools and languages—.NET, Java, PHP, Node.js.—then deploy them to a load-balanced platform that scales automatically, can be secured with Active Directory, and connects seamlessly to your on-premises resources. Azure Websites handles platform chores like availability, maintenance, and patching, so you have more time for your apps.

  • Enterprise cloud - designed for secure mission-critical applications
  • Global scale - optimized for availability and automatic scale
  • Built for DevOps - agility through continuous deployment

Whether you need a robust and high-end website for your corporate presence or a scalable and flexible enterprise-class web application, Azure Websites has what it takes to support your business. Azure Websites was designed from the ground up to accelerate development and enable continuous delivery without compromising on scale and performance.

Corporate Websites

Most Corporate Websites need to be super-responsive to visitors from all over the world, and be reliable even when there’s a huge surge of traffic that typically follows a new product launch. With Azure Websites, you can deploy your site to multiple instances and to any or all of our 13 datacenters worldwide to provide optimal response and speed to every visitor. Using our Auto-scale feature, this also guarantees making the best with your budget as your cost decreases when traffic does.

See how:
  • Create a Global Web Presence
  • How to scale websites
  • Create and deploy a website from the gallery

Enterprise Web Apps

Building enterprise-class web applications is no small feat, but with Azure Websites you have the tools needed for the job. With Active Directory integration and private network connectivity you can easily build applications catering to your employees and partners. Our PaaS platform supports the most popular technologies to make your dev work easy. Whether it’s .Net, PHP or Java, we got your back, and you can integrate with the tools you know best, such as Visual Studio, TFS and GIT to make things happen at enterprise-speed.

See how:
  • Create a secure business application on Websites
  • Get started with Azure Websites and ASP.NET
  • Create a secure ASP.NET MVC app with Membership, OAuth, and SQL Database

Digital Marketing

Creating a Digital Marketing campaign requires putting together many pieces. Social media integration and tight monitoring are the building blocks to making a campaign work, and Azure Websites provides all that, and more. With ASP.NET, you can integrate with Facebook or Twitter and embed OAUTH right into your code, and our advanced diagnostic and monitoring lets you keep your eye on the ball 24x7. With Traffic Manager Integration, you can also distribute your code to multiple datacenters to make sure it’s fully responsive no matter where your customers are.
