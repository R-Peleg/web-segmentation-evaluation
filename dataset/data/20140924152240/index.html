        Community Contest - May the best wiki win

        Sarah Manley June 15, 2010 User blog:Sarah Manley Google
        Do you have an awesome wiki you would like everyone to know about when they visit Wikia? Well now is your chance to tell us all about it!

        You have until June 29 to let us know why your community should be featured on the main page of www.wikia.com. Please use the space below to tell us, in your most creative voice, why your wiki deserves to be on Wikia.com for two weeks in July. In 250 words or less, highlight why your wiki's content, design, growth and community make it special.

        Wikis of all sizes and topics are encouraged to enter. Please remember to be a good Wikian, and don't leave any discouraging comments about other wikis. At 5:00pm Pacific time on June 29th, we will close the contest to entries. All entries must be submitted here in order to be considered. The winner will be announced in early July.

        So chat with your community about your entry, then submit it here. Remember to provide links to examples you use, and while you wait to hear who the winner is, keep improving your wiki! We look forward to hearing lots of funny, interesting tidbits about all of the amazing wikis on Wikia!

        Retrieved from "http://community.wikia.com/wiki/User_blog:Sarah_Manley/Community_Contest_-_May_the_best_wiki_win?oldid=546146"

        • Burnopedia should be featured on the main page of Wikia for a multitude of reasons. It has nearly 1000 articles making it the largest sports and racing wiki on Wikia Gaming, it has a large breadth of detailed articles on Burnout Paradise including walkthroughs on many aspects of the game (see Discovery (Burnout Paradise)), and we are working on improving our content for previous Burnout games. We use images to make the site visually appealing and easy to navigate (see Vehicles (Burnout Paradise)), and our sidebar is well-organized and has a multitude of links to different parts of the site. Burnopedia has mostly friendly and helpful users as well as five administrators/assistants who all have varying knowledge about Burnout, wikicoding, and the use of "one" in sentences. A standout feature at our wiki is the Burnopedia Clan which meets weekly to play in Burnout Paradise and attracts users who would not normally visit the site. Often our clan members turn out to be important members of our community. Burnopedia has a small but steady editing of content and a larger steady contribution to discussions about Burnout, our wiki, our clan, and other topics. Our community is currently working on Project: Rebirth to improve the visuals, content, and community infrastructure of Burnopedia. While we hate to think of Burnout players who don't know of this great source of knowledge we are excited about the prospect of new editors, ideas, and viewpoints.
